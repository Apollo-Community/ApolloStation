By playing on Apollo, users agree to follow all server rules. These rules are subject to change by discretion of the community in venues including, but not limited to, community meetings and forum threads. Rules are not up for debate within the server nor in the event of an infraction.

  1. Golden Rules
    1. Act with respect.
    2. Roleplay appropriately.
    3. Erotic content is forbidden.
    4. Listen to staff.
    5. Read the rules.


  2. Roleplay

    1. Characters must be realistic in terms of skills and abilities.
      1. Must fit niches with respect to their roles and history.
      2. Must not be omnipotent nor omniscient.
        1. Additional skills must be reasonable and noted in character's employment records.
        2. Characters can use space suits, perform CPR, and apply basic first aid.
      3. Character age should be reflected in their role, ability, and knowledge.
    2. Rounds and interactions within them are canon.
      1. Interactions, memories, or disputes that occur as a result of antag interference are excluded and not canon.
      2. Skills not related to a character's profession that are learned within a round cannot be carried over to following rounds.
    3. Characters must have an appropriate name.
      1. References to names from real life figures, characters, and media are not allowed.
      2. Names must have a minimum of a first and last name.
        1. Middle names are allowed.
        2. AI, Cyborgs, IPC's, Wryn, and Dionae are exceptions depending on lore.
      3. Honorifics such as Mr., Dr., and Capt. are not allowed.
    4. Characters do not have knowledge of antagonists nor their lore.
      1. Security and Command are only familiar with the Syndicate.
    5. Self-antagging is not allowed.
      1. Grief is not allowed.
      2. Antags are selected by the server or staff.
    6. Do not abuse voting for a shuttle call.
      1. Use of voting for shuttle calls should be limited to one call every 30 minutes.
      2. Should not be used in place of an emergency shuttle.
    7. Suicide is not allowed.
      1. Suicide is allowed with explicit permission from staff.
    8. Only logout in sleeping quarters.
      1. Heads of staff must alert server staff via adminhelp when logging out.
      2. Crew members must inform their head of staff IC if they get SSD, i.e. logout.
    9. Stealing from or harming AFK/SSD characters is not allowed.
      1. Antags must request a target change if their target is SSD.


  3. Chat

    1. English is the only language allowed in all chats.
      1. Proper English is required.
        1. This includes appropriate grammar, spelling, and capitalization
    2. In-Character (IC) chat is for speaking as a character.
      1. References outside of the lore are not allowed.
        1. This includes explicit parodies of real racism (e.g., "ligger").
      2. Erotic roleplay is forbidden.
      3. Internet shorthand (e.g. "lol", "u" instead of "you", etc.) is not allowed.
      4. Accents are allowed as long as text remains comprehensible for English speakers.
    3. Out-Of-Character (OOC) chat is for speaking as a player.
      1. Discussion of IC subjects of the current round is forbidden.
        1. Discussion of previous rounds is allowed.
      2. Racism and any other type of hate speech is forbidden.
      3. Erotic references are forbidden.
      4. Disrespect is not allowed.
      5. Offensive ckeys (BYOND usernames) are not allowed.
      6. Use of Local Out-Of-Character (LOOC) chat to ask questions or make comments about immediate IC events is allowed, but should be kept to a strict minimum.
    4. Adminhelp (Ahelp) is used for requesting staff assistance.
      1. The request must be stated explicitly.
      2. Casual conversation with staff is not allowed.
    5. Prayers are in-character messages to outside powers that may not exist.
        6. In-character rules apply, (e.g., no praying to "admins" OOC).
    6. Dead chat is used for out-of-character exchanges with players not playing in the round.
      1. Can be forced to be in-character of the game mode or event requires it.
      2. In-character style chat is allowed.
    7. Teamspeak is a voice version of Out-Of-Character chat.
      1. All Out-Of-Character rules apply.


  4. Antagonist

    1. Must make round for others.
    2. Minimize removing players from the round through killing.
      1. Killing is not allowed before twenty minutes into the round.
      2. Killing after twenty minutes is allowed within reason and purpose.
        1. Killing with explicit permission from staff removes player accountability.
      3. Mass-killing is not allowed.
      4. Killing inappropriately can be met with severe punishments.
    3. Every antag action must be roleplayed.
      1. Every antag action must have a purpose.
      2. Ganking is not allowed.
        1. The murder must be interesting and roleplayed well for the victim.
        2. Hasty murders are allowed during Red Alert.
      3. Targets must not be AFK when an antagonist acts.
    4. Must attempt to complete objective(s).
      1. Requesting for an objective change is allowed.
    5. Avoid logging off as an antagonist.
      1. Must contact staff if it is necessary to logout.

    The secret passphrase to register on the forums is: Orange ducks eat fruit salad.

  5. Staff

    1. All authority is derived from the rules.
      1. Staff are able to act in enforcing all rules.
    2. Respect to players is required.
      1. Staff being disrespected will null this as requirement, albeit still highly advised.
    3. All interferences with the round must enhance roleplay and be fun.
      1. If there are current antagonists, they must be asked before interfering with the round.
      2. Do not interfere with the round if there is currently enough activity or there exists sufficient player opposition.
        1. Votes can be called to query the players' opinions on custom events.
      3. Maximize the number of players included in events.
        1. Maximize the re-introduction of players into the round through event-related characters over staff controlled characters.
    4. Characters played by staff are subject to the same restrictions as player characters.
      1. Event characters are not restricted by the same rules.
      2. Exploiting permissions for character gain is forbidden.
    5. Cases of rule-breaking must be handled fairly.
      1. First instances of rule-breaking should be met with a warning.
        1. Griefing or any other ruin of the round for other players are exception.
      2. The offender must be allowed to briefly speak for their case.
        1. Further contesting can be forwarded to the forums after punishment is decided.
        Citation of the specific rule(s) broken must be explicit.
      3. Staff interpretation of the rules is final inside of the server.
      4. Ban type and length should be equally proportionate to the severity of the rule broken.
    6. Staff are expected to respond to all queries if they are available.
      1. If playing a critical role in the round (i.e., Head of staff, antagonist, Security), they are required to de-admin.
        1. If playing a non-critical role, it is requested that the staff member de-admin.
        2. De-admining is not required if server is short on staff in proportion to player count and work load.
    7. Staff are not to pass judgement on ban appeals for bans that they gave.
      1. Commenting on the ban appeal is acceptable.
    8. Staff are not required to answer a non-necessary request.
      1. This includes praying for items or asking to be assigned as an antag.
