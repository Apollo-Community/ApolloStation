<h2>The Golden Rules</h2>
<b>If you decide to read nothing else, read these:</b><br>
- Treat other players with respect. We&#39;re all here to have fun.<br>
- Never do any of the following: Grief, Gank, ERP, ERG, Metagame or abuse bugs.<br>
- Be aware that this is a heavy roleplaying server and you are expected to act accordingly.<br>
- Erotic content of <i>any</i> kind is immediately permabannable. There are no exceptions to this rule.<br>
- A staff member&#39;s primary role is to help you, however they are not always right. If you have an issue with a member of staff feel free to raise the issue on the forums.<br>
